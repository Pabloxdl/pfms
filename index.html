Process

How it works

Three steps, repeated every cast.

STEP_01 // CAST

Cast

Holds the cast button, then throws the rod.

STEP_02 // SHAKE

Shake

Spams a key until YOLO spots the target.

STEP_03 // FISH

Fish

Tracks the fish and holds the bar. Loses it? Recasts on its own.
